## What "Principal" Level Means in Interviews

Principal engineers are expected to operate at the **org level**, not just the team level. Interviewers test for:

- **Scope amplification** — Can you design systems that serve millions? Can your decisions unblock 10 engineers?
- **Trade-off reasoning** — Not "what is the best solution" but "what trade-offs does this solution have and why is it right here"
- **Technical depth + breadth** — Deep in JS/TS/Node, but can speak confidently about infra, data, security
- **Cross-functional influence** — Communicate technical decisions to non-technical stakeholders
- **Ownership mentality** — You own outcomes, not just tasks

---

## Interview Formats to Expect

1. **System Design** (60–90 min) — Design a large-scale system end to end
2. **Technical Deep Dive** — Walk through a past project, justify decisions
3. **Coding** (may be present) — Typically architectural coding, not leetcode
4. **Behavioral / Leadership** — STAR-format, conflict, driving change
5. **AWS / Infra** — Architecture on AWS, cost/security/scalability decisions
